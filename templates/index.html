FAQs

What is synesthesia?

Synesthesia is when two or more senses blend together — like seeing colors when you hear music, or feeling time as shapes. Our app helps you visualize those patterns.

Do I need to have synesthesia to use this?

Not at all! While it’s inspired by synesthetes, anyone can use it to explore time, numbers, and music in a more visual and creative way.

Is this scientific or just for fun?

A bit of both. The app is based on real synesthetic experiences, but it’s designed as a creative visualization tool, not a medical or diagnostic app.

Will my data or patterns be saved?

Your visualizations stay private unless you choose to save or share them. We don’t share your data without permission.

Can I share my creations with others?

Yes! You’ll be able to save and share your visualizations (coming soon).

Do I need any special setup to use it?

Nope — just a modern browser. No extra downloads or plugins required.
